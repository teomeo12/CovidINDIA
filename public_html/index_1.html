Coronavirus positive cases in India continue to rise on a daily basis despite implementation of social distancing measures and extension of nationwide lockdown. As per the recent health ministry data, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu and Delhi continue to remain the worst-affected states by the Covid-19 pandemic. However, the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) and health ministry have maintained that the community transmission of Covid-19 is not happening at the moment. The World Health Organization has even praised India’s timely actions to stop coronavirus as it has helped in minimising the spread of SARS-CoV-2 in spite of the myriad challenges.
